About the legend

  • Satyendra Nath Bose, born on January 1, 1894, in Calcutta (now Kolkata), India.

  • Developed Bose-Einstein statistics, a foundation for quantum statistics of particles

  • Pioneered the concept of Bose-Einstein condensate, a unique state of matter

  • Discovered Bose-Einstein correlations in quantum mechanics

  • Contributed to the fields of quantum mechanics, statistical mechanics, and theoretical physics

  • Derived the Planck-Bose law in blackbody radiation

  • Explored unified field theory for the unification of fundamental forces

  • Received numerous accolades and honors for his exceptional contributions
